About this guy

Hey you! yes you. Don't look around, I'm talking to you. Welcome to my portfolio! This guy is a frontend developer based in Lagos, Nigeria (and remotely by your side) who specializes in building exceptional user-interface experiences. I am focused on building accessible, human-centered products.

Having had something of an odyssey of a career, I discovered that I am in love with UI products. I am a organised person, creative thinker, problem solver, aesthetic- loving and soccer-watching 'goof off' of a guy.

I am interested in the entire frontend spectrum and hoping to extend my journey to the backend someday (baby steps bro!). I may not be the most talented out there, but I show grit and brutally achieve my goals.

I am a 'fungi' and I spread happiness (pun intended, of course) and when I am not coding, you'll find me reading a random book or playing a video game (I only know how to play FIFA) or making my friends laugh.
